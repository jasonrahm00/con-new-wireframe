CU College of Nursing Alumni Association

As a graduate of the University of Colorado College of Nursing, you automatically become a member of the CU College of Nursing Alumni Association (CONAA). Each year, the CONAA board of directors honors outstanding alumni at an awards luncheon and works with class representatives to plan alumni reunions. The History Center Committee archives nursing memorabilia and opens the Nursing History Center for events. And, of course, we raise money and give alumni scholarships each year. The support of alumni and friends is instrumental to continued success of the College of Nursing.
